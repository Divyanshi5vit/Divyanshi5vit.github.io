Projects

Hotel Management System

Semester 2


Led a team of three during our second semester and developed a menu-driven hotel management system using Python programming language and an SQL database. It was designed to automate and streamline various operations within a hotel, including managing room bookings, guest information and billing (including restaurant and gaming bill), and gave a customer discount on their bills if they were a member of the hotel.


Disease Prediction and Yoga Suggestion System

Semester 4


Group project, consisting of three other classmates, to create a web-based system for disease prediction and appropriate yoga recommendations. Our team of 4 members is utilizing HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to develop the front-end interface, while implementing a SQL database for the back-end functionalities. Our objective is to design a robust system capable of predicting diseases and suggesting appropriate yoga practices based on user inputs.
